Nestled in a cozy woodland lodge, Teddies has been serving families, friends, and travelers for generations. Our handcrafted comfort food is inspired by homemade recipes, fresh ingredients, and the warmth of gathering around one table.

Our Story

Founded by the fictional Teddy Bennett family, our restaurant began as a tiny cabin café where neighbors gathered around the fireplace after long days outdoors. Today, we continue that tradition by serving hearty meals, welcoming every guest like family, and creating memories around every table.
